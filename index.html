# Puppy Photos — How to Use This Folder This folder is your central hub for all litter photos and videos. It's organized so Claude can quickly find, sort, and use your photos for buyer emails, social media posts, and documents. --- ## Folder Structure (per litter) For each new litter, **duplicate the `_NEW LITTER TEMPLATE` folder** and rename it like this: ``` Litter Name - Month Year Example: Ruby x Scout - January 2026 ``` Inside each litter folder: | Subfolder | What goes here | |---|---| | `1 - All Photos` | Dump everything here first — straight from iCloud/iPhone | | `2 - By Puppy` | Create one subfolder per puppy (use their call name or collar color) | | `3 - Ready to Share` → `Buyer Photos` | Curated photos ready to send to a specific buyer | | `3 - Ready to Share` → `Social Media` | Edited/cropped photos ready to post | | `3 - Ready to Share` → `Documents & Ads` | Photos used in flyers, PDFs, or listings | --- ## How to Get Photos from iPhone / iCloud ### Option A — From the Mac Photos App (easiest) 1. Open the **Photos** app on your Mac 2. Click **Shared Albums** in the sidebar and open the litter's album 3. Select all photos (⌘A) or pick the ones you want 4. Go to **File → Export → Export [X] Photos...** 5. Choose the litter's `1 - All Photos` folder as the destination 6. Click **Export** ### Option B — Drag from iPhone via Finder 1. Plug your iPhone into your Mac with a USB cable 2. Open **Finder** and click your iPhone in the sidebar 3. Click the **Photos** tab 4. Drag photos directly into the `1 - All Photos` folder ### Option C — AirDrop from iPhone 1. On your iPhone, open the Photos app and select photos 2. Tap the Share icon → **AirDrop** → select your Mac 3. Photos will land in your Mac's **Downloads** folder 4. Move them into the appropriate litter's `1 - All Photos` folder --- ## Tips for Working with Claude Once photos are in this folder, just tell Claude things like: - *"Send [Puppy Name]'s best photos to [Buyer Name] at [email]"* - *"Pick 5 photos from Ruby x Scout for an Instagram post"* - *"Create a puppy availability flyer for the January litter"* - *"Move the red collar puppy photos into the By Puppy folder"* The clearer your folder and puppy names, the faster Claude can help! --- ## Naming Conventions - **Litter folders:** `[Dam] x [Sire] - [Month Year]` → `Ruby x Scout - January 2026` - **Puppy subfolders:** Use collar color or call name → `Red Collar` or `Maple` - **Shared albums on iPhone:** Match the litter folder name for easy cross-reference
